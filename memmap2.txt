007        00 000 00 3E8 #C offset

Description:
Starts adding A(1000) and B(1000) at addr 7CF (1999) and BB7 (2999) storing
the sum at C(1000) addr F9F (3999).
Then it modifies the instructions for the next iteration. It loads instr 1
and 2 from 000 and decrements their addresses by 1, then placing them back.
Then it adds the offset from B to C and modifies the address for instr 3.
It then subtracts the expected "ending state" of the AC, pre-defined on
mem location 008. This value is computed such that subtracting it from the
AC will always yield a nonnegative value until after the last iteration.
